Karri Country Good Food Inc.

Among the giant karri tree forests in Western Australia, near the southern coast at Windy Harbour, in the little town of Northcliffe, Karri Country Good Food provides fresh food directly to the public. Local growers sell fresh fruit and vegetables through the KCGF shop, which also stocks locally produced milk, cheese, eggs, bread, honey and olive oil, as well as other quality, eco-friendly products.
