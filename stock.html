Our Stock

The KCGF shop stocks a large range of locally grown food including fruit and vegetables, milk, cheese, eggs, honey and olive oil, as well as a vast range of dried foods. Some items can also be ordered on request. Just place an order at the counter and, while we offer no guarantees, we will do our best to obtain available goods for you.

Other Products

The shop also stocks a range of eco-friendly cleaning, household and personal hygiene products and locally made goods. Members can sell their produce through the shop for a small commission (20%). All sellers must be current financial members of KCGF.
